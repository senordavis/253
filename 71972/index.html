Project 3:
Brochure

Have you ever been walking through a school, the mall, or even the home improvement store, when you come across something that you wish you had more information about?

A good brochure will help you provide the customer with the information that they need to keep their interest. But be carefull, you want a brochure that will provide them with just the right amount of information. To much and they are overwhelmed. Not enough and they are frustrated. Just right and you will have a loyal brand ambassador.

For this project you will be developing a brochure for your brand and submitting it to the Brochure Dropbox by Monday, November 28 at 6:30 PM.
