Logo Design Project

So for centuries now, brands have been a part of our lives. They have helped identify businesses in ancient times like in Ostia Antica. They have helped identify who owned which cows In the American Old West, during the late 19th Century.

The reason for branding is simple. You have a product or company, and you want that product or company to have an identity that people can relate to. As people we all identify with different brands.

You as a class live in a new era where you realize that you are being marketed to directly by large corporations. When you walk down the cereal aisle at the grocery store and you are flooded with all the different brands and kinds of cereal, it is a direct result of feedback that these companies have learned from demographics that most likely include you now or when you were younger.

Same with clothing. All the different clothing companies try to make a product that you will buy. But then on top of that they market it to you with models that look good in the clothes, look like you do or want to, or even by using celebrity endorsements.

If you can wade through all the noise, you see what the successful brands are doing and how to apply it.
