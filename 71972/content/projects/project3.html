Advertising Project

Why is it that you can be sitting somewhere and then all of a sudden have a crazy craving for something, like macaroni and cheese? Doesn’t it sound so good? You know one the kind, home style mac and cheese with bread crumbs and the gooey cheese and the tasty noodles.

See now you want some of that awesomeness don’t you? Why is that? Why can seeing images and hearing certain words entice our minds to desire something? Simply put it’s all advertising.



When you talk about your company or your product, in the right way to the right people, you are communicating with the people who either have identified themselves, or that you have identified, to be people who you want to be part of the community supporting your company.

Project Specifications

For this project you must design a advertising campaign promoting your company, or a product or service that your company sells or is planning on selling.

You will be given a fictitious amount of money that will be randomly assigned. You will use this money to create your marketing plan. Your plan must outline what you will ads you will run, and for how long they will run. You must create minimally 4 pieces of advertising within your budget.

You also will need to submit finished high-resolution PDFs of a set of stationary. A minimum of three pieces (letterhead, business card, and envelope).

Save your files with appropriate names, using the following naming convention:
